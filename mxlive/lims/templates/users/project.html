MxLIVE is a web application for managing sample, shipping and experiment information related to macromolecular crystallography experiments at the synchrotron.

The lims is organized into sections accessible through the tabbed navigation above. The Samples section enables you to manage crystals, and their contents. You can organize crystals to send to the sychrotron in Containers and Dewars in the Shipping section. In the Experiments section, you can enter experiment requests, and view datasets and results from already performed experiments.
