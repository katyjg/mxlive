This is the staff portal for MxLIVE, a web application for managing sample, shipping and experiment information related to macromolecular crystallography experiments at the synchrotron.

It is organized into the following sections. The Shipping section enables you receive and manage shipments, and their contents. Experiments and their status are available in the Experiments section. In the Run Lists section, you can prepare containers for loading into the beamline automounter. Datasets and results from already performed experiments can also be inspected through the experiment links.
